ABOUT ACTOR RAM CHARAN

Ram Charan was born in Madras (now Chennai) in a Telugu family to Telugu film actor Chiranjeevi and his wife Surekha on 27 March 1985. Ram Charan's family hails from Mogalthuru and Palakollu in West Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh. His maternal grandfather was the noted comic actor Allu Ramalingaiah.
