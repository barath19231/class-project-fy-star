FY-STAR TRIVIKRAM DIRECTED MOVIES

MOVIE NAME : BHEEMLA NAYAK


Bheemla Nayak is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Saagar K Chandra from a screenplay by Trivikram Srinivas. It is an official remake of the 2020 Malayalam film Ayyappanum Koshiyum. The film stars Pawan Kalyan and Rana Daggubati in lead roles. Bheemla Nayak was filmed primarily in Hyderabad between January 2021 and February 2022 although temporarily halted by the COVID-19 pandemic. It has music composed by Thaman S with cinematography by Ravi K. Chandran and editing by Naveen Nooli. Bheemla Nayak was theatrically released on 25 February 2022 and opened to positive reviews.




RATINGS: 7.3/10
