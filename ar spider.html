FY-STAR A.R MURUGADOSS DIRECTED MOVIES

MOVIE NAME : SPYDER


Spyder is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film[5][6][7][8] written and directed by A. R. Murugadoss and produced by N. V. Prasad. The film was simultaneously shot in Telugu and Tamil languages, and stars Mahesh Babu, S. J. Suryah and Rakul Preet Singh in the lead roles. Murugadoss wrote the Tamil dialogue while the Paruchuri Brothers wrote the Telugu version. The film was edited by A. Sreekar Prasad, with cinematography by Santosh Sivan and music by Harris Jayaraj.




RATINGS: 6.3/10
